About NTT Communications HTML5 Lab

NTT Communications HTML5 Lab is the project for spreading state-of-the-art technology HTML5 and promoting new ICT HTML5 service development. As an effort of HTML5 Lab, Skyway have been developed and managed by NTT Communications.
