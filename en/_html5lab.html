About NTT Communications HTML5 Lab

NTT Communications HTML5 Lab is a project for spreading state-of-the-art HTML5 technology and promoting new ICT HTML5 service development. As part of the HTML5 Lab, Skyway was developed and is managed by NTT Communications as part of the HTML5 Lab.

NTT Communications HTML5 Lab

HTML5 Experts.jp

We manage a website about HTML5 technologies that features over 50 experts who are active domestically and internationally.

HTML5 conferences, seminars, workshops, hackathons and contests

Collaborating with the Web Engineer community, we hold and promote HTML themed events such as conferences, seminars, workshops, hackathons, and contests in order to put the technology into practice.

New HTML5 service development

We develop application, cloud, and network services that use and support HTML5 technologies. The first of these new services is SkyWay.
